# ProductCard

A full storefront product card composing [Rating](?path=/docs/components-rating--docs), [FormatBadge](?path=/docs/molecules-formatbadge--docs), [PriceBlock](?path=/docs/molecules-priceblock--docs), and [Button](?path=/docs/components-button--docs).

- **Cover** renders at a fixed 3:4 aspect ratio and lazy-loads (`loading="lazy"`).
- **Title** is clamped to two lines (`line-clamp-2`) so card heights stay aligned in grids; the full title is available via the `title` attribute.
- **Add to cart** shows the Button's loading state while an async `onAddToCart` is in flight.
