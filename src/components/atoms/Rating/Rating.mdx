# Rating

Displays a 0–5 star rating with half-star support. Full, half, and empty stars are distinguished by **shape** (filled / half-filled / outline), never colour alone — so the rating stays legible for colour-blind users and in high-contrast modes.

The whole control is a single `role="img"` with a label like `"4.5 out of 5 stars, 25 reviews"`; individual stars are hidden from assistive tech.
