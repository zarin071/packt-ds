## Accessibility

`warning` and `error` variants render with `role="alert"` (interrupts assistive tech immediately — urgent). `info` and `success` variants render with `role="status"` (announced politely, without stealing focus). The dismiss button is a real `<button>` with `aria-label="Dismiss"` and a visible focus ring.

## Tokens used
